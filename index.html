About Me

My career began on a digital marketing team, where I gained experience around managing content on eCommerce websites, analyzing data from site traffic, and deploying email marketing. There, I had the opportunity to begin applying HTML and CSS to assemble landing pages, and I became hungry to learn more about coding and web development in my spare time.

These early days of pursuing online tutorials, embracing trial & error, and rejoicing upon seeing code come to life on my screen led to my decision to pivot into full time web development. Every day is exciting, as I am advancing my skillset, training my problem solving skills, and growing in excellence as a developer.

As someone who is highly organized, detail-oriented, and teachable, I am excited to find a company to partner with as I continue to learn and grow each day.
